Paginator Component

Pagination provides navigation to the page, by sharing content across several separate pages and linking them to the bottom / top of the page. This way you give the user a sense of control by knowing where they are and they can estimate how much time it will take to find it.

When to use:

When there is a lot of content to process and the user is looking for specific information. Often used in conjunction with tables.

When not to use:

When the content is very visual.
